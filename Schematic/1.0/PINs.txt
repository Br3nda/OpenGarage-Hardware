ESP8266-WROOM-2 PIN ASSIGNMENTS:

'spare' means the pin is available
for general purpose use and mapped
out in the pin-our area.

GPIO 0: BUTTON
GPIO 2: LED
GPIO 4: SDA (spare)
GPIO 5: SCL (spare)
GPIO12: SENSOR TRIGGER
GPIO13: (spare)
GPIO14: SENSOR ECHO
GPIO15: RELAY
GPIO16: RESET (tied to reset to trigger hardware reset)
